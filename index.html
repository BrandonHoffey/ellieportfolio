Welcome to my portfolio! I'm Ellie Hoffey, a passionate photographer and cinematographer based in Denver, Colorado. I began portrait photography eight years ago and have since fallen in love with being behind the camera. With a keen eye for detail and a love for capturing both people and places natural beauty, I've honed my craft through a variety of projects, ranging from both documentary and fiction films to intimate wedding and concert photography.

My journey in photography has led me to explore filmmaking, where I aspire to work in documentary, specifically in the camera department. With each project, I strive to blend creativity, technical skill, and a deep love of learning to deliver imagery that resonates with audiences in a way that is authentic and ethical. I'm excited to embark on new adventures in the world of film and use my talents for something greater than myself.
